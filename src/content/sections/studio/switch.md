<b>Switch</b> is a monochrome puzzle platformer in which a detective is trying to find their lost cat. How do they do that? By flipping the world between black and white, of course! Even so, many puzzles stand in their way. Will you help our detective find their cat?

A tech demo of this game will be released end of august on itch. To find out more about this game, check out the itch page and follow the studio's socials to stay up to date.
