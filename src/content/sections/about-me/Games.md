Here are some of the games I have contributed to in the past years!

## Doolhof - 2025

<img src="/alette-creative-studio/images/Simple_Dice.png"
     alt="Market Meltdown screenshot"
     style="float:right; width:180px; margin-left:20px; margin-bottom:10px;">
     

[Doolhof](https://csomeoneh.itch.io/doolhof) is a game I developed for my thesis, which was themed around teaching AI literacy. In this game, players find themselves stuck in a maze and are tasked to navigating through it with the help of an old-fashioned handbook and a modern genAI called Rosie. Through gameplay the player is encouraged to interact with the AI in a critical way to escape the maze. You can find the game (in Dutch) [here](https://csomeoneh.itch.io/doolhof).
