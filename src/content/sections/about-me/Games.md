## Doolhof - 2025

<b>Doolhof</b> is a game I developed for my thesis, which was themed around teaching AI literacy. In this game, players find themselves stuck in a maze and are tasked to navigating through it with the help of an old-fashioned handbook and a modern genAI called Rosie. Through gameplay the player is encouraged to interact with the AI in a critical way to escape the maze. You can find the game (in Dutch) [here](https://csomeoneh.itch.io/doolhof).
