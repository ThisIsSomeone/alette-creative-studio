Here are some of the games I have contributed to in the past years!

## Doolhof - 2025

<img src="images/Simple_Dice.png" alt="Doolhof Logo" style="float: right; margin-left: 15px;">

[Doolhof](https://csomeoneh.itch.io/doolhof) is a game I developed for my thesis, which was themed around teaching AI literacy. In this game, players find themselves stuck in a maze and are tasked to navigating through it with the help of an old-fashioned handbook and a modern genAI called Rosie. Through gameplay the player is encouraged to interact with the AI in a critical way to escape the maze. You can find the game (in Dutch) [here](https://csomeoneh.itch.io/doolhof).

## Eden's Garden - 2024
<img src="images/mJdjf5.png" alt="Eden's Garden Logo" style="float:left; margin-right:15px;">

[Eden's Garden](https://projectedens-garden.fandom.com/wiki/Project_Eden%27s_Garden) is a murder mystery game following the style of danganronpa. I joined the team as translation engineer, to try and enable fan translations of the game.

## Market Meltdown - 2022
<img src="images/XYih7z.png" alt="Market Meltdown snippet" style="float: right; margin-left: 15px;">

[Market Meltdown](https://divergency.itch.io/market-meltdown) is a visual novel made as part of Autistic Creative Festival GameJam organised in 2022, and was coded by me! It showcases the concept of 'Sensory Overload'.
