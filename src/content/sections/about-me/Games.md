Here are some of the games I have contributed to in the past years!

## Doolhof - 2025

<div class="flex gap-5 items-start mb-6">
  <div class="flex-1">
    <a href="https://csomeoneh.itch.io/doolhof">Doolhof</a> is a game I developed for my thesis, which was themed around teaching AI literacy. In this game, players find themselves stuck in a maze and are tasked to navigating through it with the help of an old-fashioned handbook and a modern genAI called Rosie. Through gameplay the player is encouraged to interact with the AI in a critical way to escape the maze. You can find the game (in Dutch) <a href="https://csomeoneh.itch.io/doolhof">here</a>.
